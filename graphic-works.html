    Wasteland. Atlas

    2000–2001

    A monumental portfolio of etchings. Acquired by the Bibliothèque Nationale de France (Paris), the Österreichische Nationalbibliothek (Vienna), and the Bibliothèque Nationale de Luxembourg. Exhibited at the Städelsches Kunstinstitut, Frankfurt am Main; the State Tretyakov Gallery, Moscow; and institutions across Russia.

    Images coming soon

    Metropolis. Atlas

    2004

    Second portfolio in the Atlas series. Etchings on the theme of the modern city. Acquired by the Bibliothèque Nationale de France, the Österreichische Nationalbibliothek, and the Bibliothèque Nationale de Luxembourg.

    Images coming soon

    Vulcanus. Atlas

    2010

    Third and final portfolio in the Atlas series. Exhibited at the Galerie Nierendorf (Berlin), the Musée du Montparnasse (Paris), the Ashmolean Museum (Oxford), and the Fondazione Stelline (Milan). Acquired by the Bibliothèque Nationale de France, the Österreichische Nationalbibliothek, and the Bibliothèque Nationale de Luxembourg.
