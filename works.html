Artist Books

Livres d'artiste — Lithographies

Heinrich von Kleist · 2013

Hermannsschlacht

A livre d'artiste conceived around Heinrich von Kleist's drama Die Hermannsschlacht. Fifteen original lithographs — a meditation on mythology, resistance and the fate of Europe, in the tradition of Goya and Daumier.

Goethe · 2018

Faust

A livre d'artiste inspired by Goethe's Faust. Ten original lithographs — dark, monumental, saturated with the drama of Faust's pact. Each plate is a full-scale composition exploring the themes of knowledge, temptation and redemption.
