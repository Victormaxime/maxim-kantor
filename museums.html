Museums & Collections

Works by Maxim Kantor held in museum and private collections in Russia, Europe, and beyond.

Vatican · Permanent

Pontifical Academy of Sciences

Two monumental works permanently installed at the Pontifical Academy of Sciences within the Vatican: Saint Thomas Aquinas and Saint Francis and Blessed Augustine.

Germany · Permanent

Genscher Saal, Bundestag

Two monumental paintings permanently installed in the historic Genscher Saal of the Bundestag.

Private & Institutional Collections

Works by Maxim Kantor are held in major private collections in Russia, France, Germany, Italy, the United Kingdom, and the United States, as well as institutional collections including the State Russian Museum (St. Petersburg) and the State Tretyakov Gallery (Moscow).
